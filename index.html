Recommended Programs

Here are some recommended programs we have for you.

Prep For Prep

Prep for Prep is a leadership development and gifted education program founded in 1978 by Gary Simons, a public-school teacher in the Bronx. It is targeted toward high achieving minority students in New York City public, charter and parochial schools.

Seizing Every Opportunity (SEO)

Opportunities make a better world for all of us. SEO programs make all the difference.

Architecture Construction Engineering (ACE)

the ACE Mentor Program of America is a free, award-winning, afterschool program designed to attract high school students into pursuing careers in the Architecture, Construction and Engineering industry, including skilled trades. ACE is a federation of more than 70 affiliates (chapters), operating in 37 states and largely based in metropolitan areas, which deliver the program.

America On Tech (AOT)

America On Tech (AOT) has been working to create pathways for students in technology. students are not being positioned to participate in the growing technology sector and we're leaving talented, young people behind. At America On Tech, we see an opportunity to change this.
